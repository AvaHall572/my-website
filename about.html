All about me

With a specialization in cutting, styling, and coloring hair, I began my hairstyling journey at an early age. I’ve always been drawn to the beauty industry—not just for its creativity, but for the confidence and joy it brings to people. While many of my peers pursued traditional college paths, I followed a different direction, one that aligned with my passion for personal expression and helping others feel their best. From the beginning, I knew I wanted to create a space where clients could feel comfortable, seen, and empowered. My training focused on mastering modern techniques in color, design, and texture, but more importantly, it taught me how to listen. I take pride in truly understanding each client’s vision and bringing it to life through customized styles that suit their personality, lifestyle, and goals. Whether it’s a bold transformation, a soft refresh, or a detailed style for a special occasion, my goal is to make every appointment a collaborative and uplifting experience. Hair isn’t just my profession—it’s my passion. I love that every cut, every color, and every style tells a story, and I’m honored to be part of so many. Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I can’t wait to connect with you and help you feel your absolute best—one strand at a time.

School

I attended and graduated from Paul Mitchell, where I received extensive, hands-on training in both classic and modern hair techniques. From precision cutting to advanced coloring and customized styling, the program equipped me with a strong foundation in the skills every stylist needs to succeed. But what truly set my education apart was the focus on client experience, professionalism, and personal growth. At Paul Mitchell, I learned that great hairstyling isn’t just about technique—it’s about connection, communication, and confidence. We were encouraged to explore our creativity, embrace individuality, and stay ahead of beauty trends through continued education and collaboration. The environment was fast-paced, inspiring, and deeply supportive—exactly the kind of atmosphere I aim to recreate with every client I meet. The training I received helped shape not only my technical abilities but also my mindset as a stylist. Today, I bring that same level of care, creativity, and attention to detail into every service I offer. Whether it’s a classic look or a bold transformation, I approach each appointment with the same goal: to help my clients leave feeling confident, empowered, and absolutely beautiful.
