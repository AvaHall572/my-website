What services do you offer?
We specialize in women’s and men’s haircuts, color treatments, and styling. Check out my services page to see examples of my work.
How do I book an appointment?
You can book your appointment by sending me a message through my Instagram page (linked at the bottom of the page). I respond quickly to scheduling requests!
What should I bring to my appointment?
Bring any inspiration photos you like, and let me know about your hair history and any treatments you’ve had. This helps me customize your look!
How do I maintain my style between appointments?
I’ll give you tips and product suggestions to keep your hair looking great until your next visit.
Do you offer consultations?
Yes! I provide complimentary consultations to discuss your hair goals and the best approach before any major service.
